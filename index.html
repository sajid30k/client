About Us

Who Are We

We are an Independent Price Searching Company. Establish since 2015 and registered with governing bodies like the ICO. Our services cover the whole UK and our primary aim is to save all drivers (New, Experienced,Mature and Young) the most amount of money on insurance policies. We do this by searching for the cheapest prices online, with your consent on your behalf.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest level of quality services. We pledge to establish lasting relationships with our clients by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance.

Note

Please be informed that we do not act as broker, introducer or intermediary for any specific insurance companies. Based on the accuracy of the details given, our work / service is set up to providing you with free non obligatory quotes from various companies at affordable prices. It is up to you to check all details are correct before you purchase. You are strongly advised to read through our Terms & Conditions and our Privacy Policy before you proceed to accept our services. We are registered with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) and all your personal information are protected under the Data Protection Act 2018 and The EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Our Clients

Our clients count on our dependability, our drive, and our integrity and we take great pride in our accomplishments and build on them every day.
